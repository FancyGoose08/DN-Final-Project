Who are the Digital Navigators?

A Digital Navigator is someone who helps people access and use technology in their daily loves. DNs provide one-on-one support to help individuals with:

  • Tech Support

  • Access to free or low-cost devices

  • Filing out online forms

  • Referrals to digital skills classes

  • Sharing info about community resources

Digital Navigators work under MACIR to help bridge the digital devide in their communities.
